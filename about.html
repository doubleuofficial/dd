The Vision

Based in Oklahoma City, DoubleU is an independent artist and producer carving out a space within the soul-trap and melodic pain-rap landscape.

Drawing inspiration from the gritty, gospel-infused cadences of the South and the atmospheric chill of rainy urban nights, the music serves as a raw reflection of personal journey and regional identity.

Every track is an exercise in contrast—where heavy bass meets vulnerability, and cinematic visuals meet the unfiltered reality of "the 904."
